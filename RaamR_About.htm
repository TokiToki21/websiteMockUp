About Elyonbeats

His name is Elyon

In Korean, the name El Elyon means God at most high. His philosophy in music is to make music for people's heart, not their ears.

From Sacramento, California, Elyon has been honing his craft in beatmaking. He started making hip hop instrumentals when his friend suggested to listen to Nujabes, a hip hop/jazz instrumental artist. Ever since, he has been making beat instrumentals in jazz and hip-hop. His style is derived from the combination of the two genres and he now calls himself a Jazzhop producer. His style is influenced from Nujabes and Dj Premier.
